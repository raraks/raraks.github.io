Banana Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 Banana
  • Milk 300ml
  • 1 ice cube

Cooking:

  1. Peel a banana
  2. Add to Blender
  3. Pour milk
  4. Turn on the blender for 30 s
  5. Pour into a stack and add ice cubes
  6. DRINK!!!
